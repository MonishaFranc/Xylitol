Trainee Software Developer

Job Description:

  • Assist in developing software applications using the Angular framework.
  • Collaborate with senior developers to understand project requirements and specifications.
  • Write clean and efficient code following coding standards and best practices.
  • Participate in code reviews and debugging sessions to identify and fix issues.
  • Learn and apply new technologies and frameworks to enhance development skills.
  • Assist in testing and quality assurance activities to ensure software functionality and performance.
  • Support the team in documentation and technical documentation tasks.
  • Participate in training and mentoring programs to enhance technical knowledge and skills.

Requirements:

  • Currently studying in college pursuing a degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Familiarity with the Angular framework is a plus.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • A strong desire to learn and grow as a software developer.

Benefits

  • Hands-on experience working on real-world projects
  • Flexible working time models and home office
  • Opportunity to work with cutting-edge technologies
  • A workplace where you can share and implement your ideas
